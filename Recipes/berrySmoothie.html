Berry Smoothie

Berry Smoothie is beloved by those gym rats that just need their protein and calories.

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen berries
  • 3/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
Instructions
  1. Place the milk, banana, berries and yogurt in a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Add honey and protein powder, blend until mixed in.
  3. Pour into cups and enjoy!
